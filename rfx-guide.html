Introduction to RFx

RFx is an umbrella term for RFI (Request for Information), RFP (Request for Proposal), and RFQ (Request for Quote). Each stage plays a critical role in procurement processes.

RFI vs RFP vs RFQ

Aspect RFI (Request for Information) RFP (Request for Proposal) RFQ (Request for Quote)
Think of it as Tell me what's possible Show me your best solution Tell me your price
What Client Wants
  • Market research
  • Capabilities
  • No immediate buying decision
  • Complete solution
  • Value for money
  • Implementation plan
  • Exact pricing
  • Quick turnaround
  • Meets requirements
How to Respond
  • Provide high-level info
  • Share experience
  • Technical solution
  • Competitive pricing
  • Simple and direct pricing
  • Show compliance
